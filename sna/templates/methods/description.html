Timer

This method is based on well-known technic called "Pomodoro" .

The priciple as you can guess is very simple:

Everytime you decide to use social media - set up a timer with amount of time you find yourself comfortable to dozen the period of your activity online.

Disable notifications

Self-descriptive method.
The main point is to throw away any distractive signals. Depending on your phone or PC operation system the preporation proccess can differ but the key concept is the same:

Open your device settings, find application which notifications you don't want to recieve and turn them off.

Deny access to any application

This method requires more efforts as it is more radical.
It may be required to install special software or even uninstalling applications you don't want to use, while you are trying to change your habbits.
