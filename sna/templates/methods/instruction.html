Instruction

  1. Register
  2. Log in
  3. Choose method
    “Pomodoro” time restraining technique is based on the famous time management approach developed in 1980s. It was used to break work time in to intervals initially but then got widely developed by tech world for work and excessive use limit purposes.

    1. Start your 1 hour timer every time you open social media.

    2. Write down anytime beyond the timer designated time, you have spent on the social media apps having checked your daily usage time in settings.

    Methodology incorporates a notification blocking approach where the user will turn off all notifications from all social media websites. This will reduce the distracting notification and therefore remove the incentive to open the platform instantaneously.

    1. Go to the settings.

    2. Open “Notifications” tab in settings.

    3. Open the desired app.

    4. Turn off notification.

    The simplest and the hardest method out of the three. Simply delete all the social media apps from your device. If the user has decided to download the app back, he is asked to document his usage.

    1. Go to your Home Screen.

    2. Choose social media app.

    3. Delete the app.

  4. Summarize all the usage data from the described social media applications in the welcome email.

  5. Submit
  6. Continue for 30 days.
Note

All the above methodologies were selected because of the simplicity of their use, and minimal effort from the studied subjects.
In practice those methodologies can be used separately or they can used in combination with one another.
